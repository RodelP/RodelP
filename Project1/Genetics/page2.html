Green Fisher

This is the default color of a fischer lovebird and the main original form of Agapornis fischeri.

Sable Fischer

Sable Fischer is the next mutation that occur in the philippines

Green Fischer Opaline

This is the current mutation available in the philippines looks like with scale like coloration in the feather and still undergoing mutation on different colors.

Green Euwing Opaline

Euwing mutation are lovebird with yellow feather coloration (for green series) and white feather (for blue series) in the whole body and visible in the entire back

Parblue

This mutation appear only in blue series lovebird with light yellow color in the head.

This is a personata with parblue and opaline mutation
