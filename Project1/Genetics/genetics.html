The three mutations of Agapornis Roseicollis

Whiteface

Green whiteface roseicollis lovebird as the name indicate a bird with white facial color and now the original face color mutated to cover the entire head which is called Opaline.

Peachface

This is the default mutation for Roseicollis meaning if paired to orange face or whiteface as long as the peach don't carry any of the other genes the result would be peachface. Also has the opaline mutation

Orangeface

This is olivegreen orangeface roseicollis lovebird with a orange coloration in the face and also mutated to opaline.
